One PhD student (4-year position) and two postdocs (3-year positions) to work on logic and machine learning are offered at the University of Helsinki:

- PhD: [Doctoral Researcher in Logical Reasoning and Machine Learning](https://jobs.helsinki.fi/job/Helsinki-Doctoral-Researcher-in-Logical-Reasoning-and-Machine-Learning/1349802357/)
- Postdoc 1: [Postdoctoral Researcher in Logical Reasoning and Machine Learning](https://jobs.helsinki.fi/job/Helsinki-Postdoctoral-Researcher-in-Logical-Reasoning-and-Machine-Learning/1349801657/)

- Postdoc 2: [Postdoctoral Researcher in Logical Reasoning and Machine Learning (ARC Challenge)](https://jobs.helsinki.fi/job/Helsinki-Postdoctoral-Researcher-in-Logical-Reasoning-and-Machine-Learning-%28ARC-Challenge%29/1349801957/)

The PhD and Postdoc 1 positions are on fundamental research in logic-based machine learning, focusing on using SAT/ASP/MaxSAT solvers.

Postdoc 2 focuses on developing logic-based methods for the Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC), also using solvers.

There are also postdoc positions funded via [ELLIS Finland](https://www.ellisinstitute.fi/postdoc-recruit-2026).

Please feel to email Andrew Cropper for more information: `andrew.cropper@helsinki.fi`.
